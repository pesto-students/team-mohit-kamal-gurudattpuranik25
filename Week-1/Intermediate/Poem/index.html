Haveri, Karnataka, India

Location

Haveri is a city in Karnataka, India. It is the administrative headquarters of Haveri district. Haveri is famous for its cardamom garlands and Byadagi red chillies.

About

Weather

Its mostly sunny throught the year.

Population

The population is around 91000.

Sports

Volley ball, kabbaddi and kho-kho are popular sports in Haveri.
